- Retrieved populated paper cards from the 2025 and 2026 `Poster` and `Oral` schedule pages.

- Deduplicated by `(year, lowercase title)`, preferring `Oral` over `Poster` when a paper appeared on both pages.
- Caveat: the accessible `type=Spotlight` schedule pages returned only placeholder cards during this session, so this artifact contains verified `Oral` and `Poster` labels only and records the spotlight-page observation separately.
