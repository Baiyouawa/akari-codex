## Quick takeaways

1. ICML 2024 leans more heavily toward MARL theory and coordination structure than toward LLM-based multi-agent systems.
   - Provenance: the 2024 section above contains 7 clearly MARL/theory-oriented papers among 11 listed entries.

2. ICML 2025 shows a visible increase in LLM-agent system papers and evaluation/benchmark work.
   - Provenance: entries 12, 13, 14, 16, 18, 21, and 24 are LLM-agent system or benchmark/evaluation oriented, all from the 2025 section.

3. ICML 2025 includes explicit benchmark/evaluation artifacts relevant to the project open question on multi-agent evaluation.
   - Provenance: `Windows Agent Arena`, `OmniBench`, and `Ad-Hoc Human-AI Coordination Challenge` in entries 13, 14, and 22.
